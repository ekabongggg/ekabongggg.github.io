ABOUT ME
I'm Erica Joyce B. Bulayan.
Born in January 13, 2000.
22 years old.
A BSIT student in PLSP.
You can call me EJ for short and that's my nickname. It sounded like a boy name, but I'm actually simple girl who loves taking pictures. Either it's a selfie, portraits and any other type and ways of taking photos or what we called moments. I started liking photography since when I was in grade school. Everytime that there is an activity or any event in our school, I captured every happy happenings and for me it's a memories. That's why every photo that I take is important and valuable for me.


What I like the most is capturing persons with different emotions or reactions especially when they are smiling and happy. I'm really curious if the reactions that they showed was true, sincere or not. Why? It's because there are people that smiling, acting happy, act like they enjoy the moment, but actually they are not deep inside.


The part that gives me peace in photography is when I captured the beauty of a place especially when I'm visiting nature-friendly places like Tagaytay City with a nice view. I love taking pictures of the sunsets, sunrises, mountains, the color of the nature calms my mind and my whole body. When I'm stress, sad, feeling lonely, I just looked at the pictures that I captured and it relaxes my mind. Why is that? Because it is so beautiful, like life. Sometimes we feel empty, sometimes we feel sad or happy just like the nature, but in the end, we are still fighting and going on with our life like the nature that we see, they continue to grow even if there are some who destorys them. That's why we have to love and cherish the nature just like how we love our life.


I'm not yet a professional or good enough in photography, but I hope that you understand and liked why I love taking photos.
